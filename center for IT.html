Center for IT (CIT)

Our Mission

To complement the mission of the NIIBS by scientific discovery and preservation of knowledge rooted in Buddhist principles with scientific reasoning and dissemination of knowledge of Buddhist principles to the global village with the use of technology.

CIT will foster lifelong learning, critical thinking and partner with like-minded communities spanning the globe in firthering human endeavors that serve all mankind ysing science and technology behind Buddhist teachings and practices.
